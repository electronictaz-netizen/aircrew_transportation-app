## Getting Started

### Step 1: Receive Your Invitation

Your company administrator will send you an invitation email with:
- A link to access the app
- Your login email address
- Instructions to create your account

### Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Click the link in your invitation email
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your email address (the one from the invitation)
4. Create a secure password (at least 8 characters)
5. Complete the sign-up process

**Important**: Use the exact email address from your invitation.

### Step 3: Log In

1. Go to the app URL (provided by your company)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll automatically see your **Driver Dashboard** with all your assigned trips.

## Understanding Trip Information

Each trip card displays important information:

### Airport

The airport where you'll pick up or drop off. The airport name and code will be displayed on each trip card. Your company may use one or more airports depending on your service area.

### Flight Number

The airline and flight number (e.g., UA1234, DL5678)

### Pickup Date & Time

- **Date**: When to pick up the passenger
- **Time**: Exact pickup time
- **Location**: Where to pick up (airport, address, or saved location)

### Dropoff Location

Where to take the passenger (airport, address, or saved location)

### Passenger Information

- Passenger name
- Contact information (if provided)
- Special instructions (if any)

### Special Instructions

Important notes such as:
- Wheelchair assistance needed
- Multiple passengers
- Special vehicle requirements
- Contact preferences

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications to keep you informed about your assignments. Here's what to expect:

### Notification Types

#### 1. New Trip Assignment

**When you receive it:**
- Immediately when a trip is assigned to you
- When a trip is reassigned from another driver to you

**What it contains:**
- Flight number
- Pickup date and time
- Pickup location
- Basic trip information

**What to do:**
1. Check your Driver Dashboard
2. Review the full trip details
3. Confirm you can complete the trip
4. Contact your manager if you have conflicts

**Notification methods:**
- **Email**: Full trip details sent to your email
- **SMS/Text**: Brief summary sent to your phone
- **In-App**: Browser notification (if enabled)

#### 2. Trip Reassignment

**When you receive it:**
- When a trip is moved from you to another driver
- When a trip is moved from another driver to you

**What it contains:**
- Notification that the trip has been reassigned
- New driver information (if you're being removed)
- Trip details (if you're being assigned)

**What to do:**
- If removed: Remove the trip from your schedule
- If assigned: Add the trip to your schedule and review details

#### 3. Daily Assignment Summary

**When you receive it:**
- Usually sent in the evening for the following day
- Sent by your administrator or manager

**What it contains:**
- List of all trips for tomorrow
- Pickup times
- Pickup locations
- Basic trip information

**What to do:**
1. Review all assignments for tomorrow
2. Plan your route
3. Check the app for full details
4. Contact your manager with any questions

**Notification methods:**
- **Email**: Detailed summary with all trips
- **SMS/Text**: Brief summary with times and locations

**Example Email:**
```
Daily Assignment Summary - January 16, 2025

You have 3 trips scheduled for tomorrow:

1. Flight UA1234
   Pickup: 2:00 PM
   Location: Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF)
```

**Example SMS:**
```
Tomorrow's trips: UA1234 @ 2PM, DL5678 @ 5:30PM, AA9012 @ 8PM. Check app for details.
```

### Notification Preferences

You can set how you receive notifications:

- **Email Only**: Receive notifications via email
- **SMS/Text Only**: Receive notifications via text message
- **Both**: Receive notifications via both email and SMS

**To change your preferences:**
Contact your administrator or manager - they can update your notification settings.

### Understanding Notification Content

**Basic Information** (in daily summaries):
- Flight numbers
- Pickup times
- Pickup locations

**Full Information** (in assignment notifications):
- Complete passenger details
- Exact addresses
- Special instructions
- Contact information

**Remember**: Daily summaries show basic info. Always check the app for complete trip details before your pickup time.

## Checking Flight Status

### Why Check Flight Status?

Flights can be delayed, cancelled, or arrive early. Checking flight status helps you:
- Arrive on time
- Avoid waiting unnecessarily
- Adjust your schedule if needed

### How to Check Flight Status

1. **Find the trip** in your Driver Dashboard
2. **Click "Check Flight Status"** on the trip card
3. **View the information**:
   - Current flight status (On Time, Delayed, Cancelled, etc.)
   - Scheduled arrival time
   - Actual/Estimated arrival time
   - Gate information (if available)

### Important Notes

âš ï¸ **Cost Warning**: Excessive use of flight status checks may incur additional costs for your company. Only check when necessary.

**When to check:**
- Before leaving for pickup (especially for early morning flights)
- If you suspect a delay
- When you arrive at the airport
- If the passenger contacts you about delays

**When NOT to check:**
- Multiple times for the same flight
- For flights that are clearly on time
- Just out of curiosity

### What to Do If Flight is Delayed

1. **Note the new arrival time**
2. **Adjust your schedule** if needed
3. **Contact the passenger** if you have their information
4. **Update your manager** if the delay is significant
5. **Wait at the airport** or return at the new time

### What to Do If Flight is Cancelled

1. **Contact your manager immediately**
2. **Do not proceed to the airport**
3. **Wait for instructions** on whether to pick up the passenger on a later flight
4. **Update your schedule** accordingly

## Mobile App Installation

### Install the App on Your Phone

The app works on mobile devices and can be installed like a native app:

#### For iPhone (iOS):

1. Open the app in Safari browser
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

#### For Android:

1. Open the app in Chrome browser
2. Tap the **Menu** (three dots)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Add"** or **"Install"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### Benefits of Installing

- **Quick access** - Open the app with one tap
- **Works offline** - Some features work without internet
- **Faster loading** - Better performance than browser
- **Notifications** - Receive push notifications (if enabled)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Forgot password or can't log in

**Solution**:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Not Seeing My Trips

**Problem**: Dashboard shows no trips

**Possible causes**:
- No trips assigned to you yet
- Trips are for a different date
- You're logged in with the wrong account

**Solution**:
1. Check with your manager to confirm assignments
2. Verify you're logged in with the correct email
3. Check if trips are scheduled for today or tomorrow

### Notifications Not Working

**Problem**: Not receiving email or text notifications

**Possible causes**:
- Email address or phone number incorrect
- Notifications disabled in your profile
- Email going to spam folder

**Solution**:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify your email and phone number with your manager
3. Ask your manager to check your notification preferences
4. Ensure notifications are enabled in your phone settings

### Flight Status Not Updating

**Problem**: Can't check flight status or getting errors

**Possible causes**:
- Flight number entered incorrectly
- Flight not found in the system
- Temporary service issue

**Solution**:
1. Verify the flight number is correct
2. Try checking again in a few minutes
3. Contact your manager if the issue persists
4. Check the airline's website directly as a backup

### App Not Loading

**Problem**: App won't load or shows errors

**Solution**:
1. **Refresh the page** (pull down on mobile, F5 on desktop)
2. **Clear browser cache**:
   - iPhone: Settings â†’ Safari â†’ Clear History and Website Data
   - Android: Chrome â†’ Settings â†’ Privacy â†’ Clear Browsing Data
3. **Try a different browser**
4. **Check your internet connection**
5. **Contact support** if the problem continues

### Can't See Full Trip Details

**Problem**: Trip card doesn't show all information

**Solution**:
1. Click on the trip card to expand details
2. Scroll down to see all information
3. Check if you're viewing on mobile (some details may be hidden in the card view)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I know if I have new trips?

**A**: You'll receive a notification (email, SMS, or both) when a trip is assigned to you. You can also check your Driver Dashboard anytime.

### Q: What if I can't complete an assigned trip?

**A**: Contact your manager immediately. They can reassign the trip to another driver.

### Q: Do I need to check in when I complete a trip?

**A**: Check with your manager about your company's procedures. The app shows your assignments, but check-in procedures may vary.

### Q: Can I see past trips?

**A**: The dashboard shows current and upcoming trips. Past trips are typically removed from view. Contact your manager if you need to see past trip history.

### Q: What if the passenger doesn't show up?

**A**: 
1. Wait a reasonable amount of time (check with your company's policy)
2. Try to contact the passenger if you have their information
3. Contact your manager
4. Follow your company's no-show procedures

### Q: How often should I check the app?

**A**: 
- **Daily**: Check in the morning and evening
- **Before each trip**: Verify details and check flight status
- **When notified**: Check immediately when you receive a notification

### Q: Can I use the app offline?

**A**: Some features work offline, but you'll need internet to:
- View new assignments
- Check flight status
- Receive notifications

### Q: What if my phone battery dies?

**A**: 
- Keep a car charger in your vehicle
- Charge your phone between trips
- Have backup directions (written or printed)
- Contact your manager if you can't access the app

### Q: How do I update my contact information?

**A**: Contact your manager or administrator. They can update your email address, phone number, and notification preferences.

### Q: What if I receive a notification for a trip I already completed?

**A**: This might be a duplicate notification or system delay. Check your dashboard to confirm. If the trip is already completed, you can ignore the notification or contact your manager.
