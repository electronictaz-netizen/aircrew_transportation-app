## Accessing the Portal

### Portal URL

Access the Customer Portal at:
```
https://onyxdispatch.us/portal/{BOOKING_CODE}
```

Where `{BOOKING_CODE}` is your transportation provider's booking code. You'll receive this code from your provider.

### Alternative Access Methods

- **Direct Link**: Your provider may send you a direct link
- **Query Parameter**: `https://onyxdispatch.us/portal?code={BOOKING_CODE}`
- **Company Website**: Many providers include portal links on their website

### Logging In

#### Step 1: Enter Your Information

1. **Open the portal link** in your web browser
2. **Enter your email address** OR **phone number**
   - Use the exact email or phone number your provider has on file
   - You only need to provide one (email OR phone)
3. **Click "Continue"**

#### Step 2: Verify with Access Code

1. **Check your email or phone** for a 6-character access code
   - Example: `A3B9X2`
   - Codes are case-sensitive
2. **Enter the access code** in the portal
3. **Click "Verify Code"**

### Login Tips

- **Bookmark the Portal**: Save the portal link for quick access
- **Use Consistent Information**: Always use the same email/phone
- **Check Spam Folder**: Access codes may go to spam/junk
- **Request New Code**: If code doesn't work, go back and request a new one

## Managing Your Trips

### Viewing Upcoming Trips

1. **Click "Upcoming Trips" tab** (default view when you log in)
2. **Browse your scheduled trips**
   - Each trip shows all relevant details
   - Trips are sorted by date (soonest first)

### Trip Information Displayed

Each trip card shows:
- **Flight Number** or **Trip Identifier**
- **Date & Time**: Pickup date and time
- **Locations**: Pickup and dropoff addresses
- **Passengers**: Number of passengers
- **Driver**: Driver name (if assigned)
- **Status**: Current trip status

### Understanding Trip Statuses

- **Unassigned**: Trip is scheduled but no driver assigned yet
- **Assigned**: A driver has been assigned to your trip
- **In Progress**: Your trip is currently happening
- **Completed**: Trip has been finished

### Viewing Trip History

1. **Click "Trip History" tab**
2. **View all past trips**
   - See completed trips
   - Access receipts and ratings
   - Review trip details

### Trip History Features

- **Complete Records**: All your past trips in one place
- **Receipt Access**: Download receipts for any completed trip
- **Rating Options**: Rate trips you've completed
- **Search & Filter**: Find specific trips easily

## Downloading Receipts & Invoices

### How to Download a Receipt

1. **Navigate to "Trip History" tab**
2. **Find the trip** you need a receipt for
3. **Click "üìÑ Download Receipt"** button
4. **Receipt downloads** as an HTML file
5. **Open the file** in any web browser
6. **Print or save** as needed

### Receipt Information Included

Your receipt contains:
- **Customer Information**: Your name, email, and phone
- **Trip Details**: Date, time, pickup and dropoff locations
- **Trip ID**: Unique identifier for your records
- **Status**: Trip completion status
- **Passenger Count**: Number of passengers
- **Pricing**: Trip rate and charges (if applicable)
- **Company Information**: Transportation provider details

### Printing Receipts

1. **Open the downloaded receipt** in your browser
2. **Press Ctrl+P** (Windows) or **Cmd+P** (Mac)
3. **Select your printer**
4. **Adjust print settings** if needed
5. **Click Print**

### Receipt Tips

- **Download Immediately**: Get receipts right after trips
- **Save for Records**: Keep receipts for expense reports or taxes
- **Email to Yourself**: Forward receipts to your email for backup
- **Print Multiple Copies**: Print extras if needed for reimbursement

## Requesting Trip Changes

### When Can You Request Changes?

You can request changes for:
- ‚úÖ **Upcoming trips only**
- ‚úÖ Trips that are **not yet completed**
- ‚úÖ Changes requested **in advance** (not same-day)

### Types of Changes You Can Request

- **Date Changes**: Modify the pickup date
- **Time Changes**: Adjust the pickup time
- **Location Changes**: Change pickup or dropoff location
- **Passenger Changes**: Update number of passengers
- **Other Changes**: Any other modifications needed

### How to Request Changes

1. **Go to "Upcoming Trips" tab**
2. **Find the trip** you want to modify
3. **Click "‚úèÔ∏è Request Changes"** button
4. **Select change type** from the dropdown:
   - Date
   - Time
   - Location
   - Passengers
   - Other
5. **Describe the changes** you need in detail
6. **Provide a reason** for the change
7. **Click "Submit Request"**

### What Happens After You Submit

1. **Request Received**: Your request is sent to your transportation provider
2. **Provider Review**: They review your request (usually within 24 hours)
3. **Provider Contact**: You'll be contacted to confirm or discuss changes
4. **Trip Updated**: If approved, your trip will be updated

### Important Notes About Changes

- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Not Automatic**: Changes must be approved by your provider
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Contact Directly**: For urgent changes, call your provider immediately
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Same-Day Changes**: May not be possible for same-day modifications
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Availability**: Changes depend on driver and vehicle availability

### Best Practices

- **Request Early**: Submit change requests as soon as possible
- **Be Specific**: Provide detailed information about what needs to change
- **Explain Reason**: Help your provider understand why the change is needed
- **Follow Up**: If urgent, call after submitting the request

## Rating & Reviewing Trips

### Why Your Feedback Matters

Your ratings and reviews help:
- Improve service quality
- Recognize excellent drivers
- Address concerns quickly
- Enhance the experience for all customers

### How to Rate a Trip

1. **Go to "Trip History" tab**
2. **Find a completed trip** you want to rate
3. **Click "‚≠ê Rate Trip"** button
4. **Complete the rating form**:
   - **Overall Rating**: 1-5 stars (required)
   - **Driver Rating**: 1-5 stars (if driver was assigned)
   - **Vehicle Rating**: 1-5 stars (optional)
   - **Review**: Write your feedback (optional)
   - **Would Recommend**: Check if you'd recommend the service
5. **Click "Submit Rating"**

### Rating Guidelines

- **5 Stars**: Excellent service, exceeded expectations
- **4 Stars**: Very good service, minor issues
- **3 Stars**: Good service, met expectations
- **2 Stars**: Below expectations, some issues
- **1 Star**: Poor service, significant problems

### Writing Reviews

**Helpful reviews include:**
- What went well
- Areas for improvement
- Specific details about your experience
- Driver name (if applicable)
- Constructive feedback

**Example Review:**
> "Excellent service! Driver John was punctual, professional, and helpful with luggage. Vehicle was clean and comfortable. Would definitely use again."

### Your Feedback is Valued

- Ratings are shared with drivers and management
- Reviews help improve service quality
- Your input makes a difference
- All feedback is appreciated!

## Troubleshooting

### Portal Won't Load

**Possible Causes:**
- Internet connection issues
- Incorrect portal link
- Browser compatibility
- Server maintenance

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Verify the portal link is correct
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact your transportation provider

### Can't Log In

**"No account found" Error:**
- Verify you're using the correct email/phone number
- Check for typos in your email/phone
- Ensure portal access is enabled (contact your provider)
- Verify your account is active

**"Invalid access code" Error:**
- Access codes can only be used once
- Request a new code by going back and trying again
- Check that you entered the code correctly (case-sensitive)
- Codes expire after use

**Didn't Receive Access Code:**
- Check your spam/junk email folder
- Verify your email/phone number is correct
- Wait a few minutes and try again
- Contact your transportation provider

### Can't See My Trips

**Possible Reasons:**
- No trips assigned to your account
- Trips are for a different customer account
- Portal access not enabled
- Trips belong to a different company

**Solutions:**
- Contact your transportation provider
- Verify your email/phone matches their records
- Ask them to check your account status
- Confirm trips are assigned to your customer ID

### Receipt Won't Download

**Check:**
- Browser pop-up blocker is disabled
- Downloads are allowed in browser settings
- Sufficient storage space on device
- Browser is up to date

**Solutions:**
- Allow pop-ups for onyxdispatch.us
- Check browser download settings
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache
- Check available storage space

### Can't Request Changes

**Possible Reasons:**
- Trip is already completed
- Trip is currently in progress
- Request feature not available for this trip
- Trip is too close to departure time

**Solutions:**
- Only upcoming trips can be modified
- Contact provider directly for urgent changes
- Use phone or email for immediate needs
- Submit requests well in advance

### Other Issues

**If you encounter other problems:**
- Try refreshing the page
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different device
- Contact your transportation provider

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my email/phone in the portal?**
A: No, contact your transportation provider to update your contact information.

**Q: How long are access codes valid?**
A: Access codes expire after use. Request a new code if needed.

**Q: Can I cancel a trip through the portal?**
A: No, contact your provider directly to cancel trips.

**Q: Can I book new trips through the portal?**
A: No, the portal is for viewing existing trips only. Use the booking portal or contact your provider for new bookings.

**Q: Can I see trips from multiple companies?**
A: No, each portal access is tied to one transportation provider.

**Q: How do I update my account information?**
A: Contact your transportation provider to update your name, email, phone, or other account details.

**Q: What if I forget my portal link?**
A: Contact your transportation provider to get your portal link again.

**Q: Can I access the portal on mobile?**
A: Yes, the portal works on mobile devices through your web browser.
