## Notifications You'll Receive

### 1. New Trip Assignment
- **When**: Immediately when assigned
- **Contains**: Flight, time, location
- **Action**: Check app for full details

### 2. Trip Reassignment  
- **When**: Trip moved to/from you
- **Action**: Update your schedule

### 3. Daily Summary
- **When**: Evening (for tomorrow)
- **Contains**: All trips for next day
- **Action**: Review and plan route

**Methods**: Email, SMS, or Both (set by manager)

## What to Do If...

### Flight is Delayed
- Note new arrival time
- Adjust schedule
- Contact passenger (if info provided)
- Update manager

### Flight is Cancelled
- Contact manager immediately
- Don't go to airport
- Wait for instructions

### Passenger Doesn't Show
- Wait reasonable time
- Try to contact passenger
- Contact manager
- Follow company no-show policy

### Can't Complete Trip
- Contact manager immediately
- They can reassign
