# Subscription and Trial Guide

## Overview

The Onyx Transportation App offers flexible subscription plans to meet your business needs. All new companies can start with a **14-day free trial** to explore all features before committing to a paid plan.

## Free Trial

### Starting Your Free Trial

When you sign up through the "Start Free Trial" link on our website (`tazsoftware.biz`), you automatically receive:

- **14 days** of full access
- **Basic tier features** during the trial period
- **No credit card required**
- **Automatic setup** - no configuration needed

### During Your Trial

1. **Trial Banner**: You'll see a banner at the top of your dashboard showing:
   - Days remaining in your trial
   - Reminder to subscribe before trial ends
   - Direct link to subscription management

2. **Full Feature Access**: During your trial, you have access to all Basic tier features:
   - Unlimited trips
   - Location management
   - Custom fields
   - Standard reports
   - Email support

3. **Subscribe Anytime**: You can subscribe at any point during your trial. Your subscription will begin immediately, and you'll be billed from that date.

### Trial Expiration

- **3 Days Before Expiration**: You'll see a warning banner reminding you to subscribe
- **On Expiration**: Your account automatically downgrades to the Free tier
- **After Expiration**: You'll see an alert banner prompting you to subscribe to continue using premium features

### What Happens After Trial Expires?

If you don't subscribe before your trial ends:
- Your account automatically moves to the **Free tier**
- You'll have access to:
  - Up to 10 trips per month
  - Basic trip management
  - Driver management
  - Basic reports
- Premium features (unlimited trips, custom fields, location management) will be locked
- You can subscribe at any time to regain full access

## Managing Your Subscription

### Accessing Subscription Management

1. Log in to your Management Dashboard
2. Click **Configuration** in the header
3. Select **Subscription Management**

### Viewing Your Current Plan

In Subscription Management, you'll see:
- Your current plan name and price
- Subscription status (Active, Trialing, etc.)
- Renewal date or trial end date
- Features included in your plan

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Subscription Management**
2. Review available plans
3. Click **"Upgrade to [Plan Name]"** on the plan you want
4. You'll be redirected to Stripe Checkout
5. Enter your payment information
6. Complete the checkout process
7. Your subscription will be activated immediately

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Subscription Management**
2. Click **"Downgrade to [Plan Name]"** on a lower-tier plan
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe Customer Portal
4. Confirm the change
5. Your plan will change at the end of your current billing period

### Managing Billing

1. Go to **Subscription Management**
2. Click **"Manage Billing"**
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe Customer Portal where you can:
   - Update payment methods
   - View billing history
   - Download invoices
   - Cancel your subscription

### Canceling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Subscription Management**
2. Click **"Manage Billing"**
3. In the Stripe Customer Portal, select **Cancel Subscription**
4. Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period
5. After cancellation, your account will automatically move to the Free tier

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Trial Questions

**Q: Do I need a credit card to start a free trial?**  
A: No, the free trial requires no credit card. You can explore all features risk-free.

**Q: What happens if I don't subscribe before my trial ends?**  
A: Your account automatically moves to the Free tier. You can subscribe at any time to regain premium features.

**Q: Can I extend my trial?**  
A: Trial periods are fixed at 14 days. If you need more time, contact support at support@tazsoftware.biz.

**Q: Will I lose my data if I don't subscribe?**  
A: No, all your data is preserved. You'll just have limited access to features on the Free tier.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**  
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through Subscription Management.

**Q: When will I be charged?**  
A: You're charged immediately when you subscribe. After that, you're billed monthly on the same date.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: We accept all major credit cards through Stripe's secure payment processing.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**  
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: All your data is preserved. Your account moves to the Free tier, and you can resubscribe anytime to regain premium features.

**Q: Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?**  
A: No, there are no setup fees or hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay.

### Feature Questions

**Q: What happens to my trips if I exceed the Free tier limit?**  
A: You'll see a message when trying to create a trip beyond your limit. You'll need to upgrade to continue creating trips.

**Q: Can I use custom fields I created if I downgrade?**  
A: Custom field data is preserved, but you won't be able to edit or create new custom fields on the Free tier.

**Q: Will I lose saved locations if I downgrade?**  
A: Saved locations are preserved, but you won't be able to add new locations on the Free tier.

### Common Issues

**Issue**: Can't access Subscription Management  
**Solution**: Make sure you're logged in as an Administrator or Manager. Only these roles can manage subscriptions.

**Issue**: Payment failed  
**Solution**: Check your payment method in the Stripe Customer Portal. Update your card if needed.

**Issue**: Features locked but I have a subscription  
**Solution**: Refresh your browser or log out and log back in. If the issue persists, contact support.

## Best Practices

1. **Subscribe Before Trial Ends**: Avoid interruption by subscribing a few days before your trial expires
2. **Choose the Right Plan**: Start with Basic if you're unsure - you can always upgrade later
3. **Monitor Your Usage**: Keep track of your trip count on the Free tier to avoid hitting limits
4. **Keep Payment Updated**: Ensure your payment method is current to avoid service interruption
5. **Review Your Plan Regularly**: As your business grows, consider upgrading to Premium for advanced features
