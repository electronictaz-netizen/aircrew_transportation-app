## Getting Started

### Step 1: Sign Up for Free Trial

**New Companies**: Start with a 14-day free trial!

1. Visit **https://tazsoftware.biz**
2. Click **"Start Free Trial"**
3. You'll be redirected to the app sign-up page
4. Create your account with:
   - Your email address
   - A secure password
5. Your **14-day free trial** starts automatically with Basic tier features

**Note**: No credit card required for the trial. You'll have full access to Basic tier features for 14 days.

### Step 2: Receive Your Invitation (For Team Members)

If you're joining an existing company, your administrator will send you an invitation email containing:
- Your login credentials
- A link to access the application
- Your assigned role (Admin, Manager, or Driver)

### Step 3: Sign Up / Log In

1. Click the link in your invitation email, or navigate to the application URL
2. If this is your first time:
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Enter your email address (the one you received the invitation for)
   - Create a secure password
   - Complete the sign-up process
3. If you already have an account:
   - Click "Sign In"
   - Enter your email and password

### Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll be taken to your dashboard based on your role:
- **Administrators & Managers**: Management Dashboard
- **Drivers**: Driver Dashboard

### Step 5: Understand Your Subscription

- **On Trial**: You'll see a banner showing days remaining. Subscribe anytime during your trial.
- **Free Tier**: Limited to 10 trips/month. Upgrade anytime for unlimited trips and premium features.
- **Paid Subscription**: Full access to all features in your plan tier.

**For detailed subscription information, see [SUBSCRIPTION_AND_TRIAL_GUIDE.md](./SUBSCRIPTION_AND_TRIAL_GUIDE.md)**

## User Roles

The system has three user roles, each with different permissions:

### Administrator
- Full access to all features
- Can manage company settings
- Can create and manage trips
- Can manage drivers and locations
- Can invite new users
- Can assign trips to drivers

### Manager
- Can create and manage trips
- Can view and manage drivers
- Can assign trips to drivers
- Can view company reports
- Cannot manage company settings or users

### Driver
- Can view assigned trips
- Can check flight status
- Can view trip details and directions
- Receives notifications for new assignments

## For Administrators

### Managing Your Company

1. **Access Company Settings**
   - Click "Company Settings" in the Management Dashboard
   - Update company name, logo, and display settings

2. **Manage Subscription**
   - Click "Configuration" → "Subscription Management"
   - View your current plan and subscription status
   - Upgrade, downgrade, or manage billing
   - See trial expiration date and days remaining

2. **Invite New Users**
   - Go to the Admin Dashboard (if you have admin access)
   - Select your company
   - Click "+ Invite User"
   - Enter the user's email and select their role
   - Click "Send Invitation"
   - The user will be automatically linked when they sign up

### Creating Trips

1. Click "Create Trip" in the Management Dashboard
2. Fill in the trip details:
   - **Airport**: Select the airport (BUF, ROC, SYR, or ALB)
   - **Flight Number**: Enter the flight number
   - **Pickup Date & Time**: When to pick up the passenger
   - **Pickup Location**: Choose from airport, saved location, or enter custom address
   - **Dropoff Location**: Choose from airport, saved location, or enter custom address
   - **Passenger Information**: Name and contact details
   - **Special Instructions**: Any special requirements
3. Click "Save Trip"

### Managing Drivers

1. Click "Manage Drivers" in the Management Dashboard
2. **Add a Driver**:
   - Click "Add Driver"
   - Enter driver details (name, email, phone, license number)
   - Set notification preferences (Email or Both)
   - Click "Save"
3. **Edit a Driver**:
   - Click "Edit" next to the driver
   - Update information as needed
   - Click "Save"
4. **Deactivate a Driver**:
   - Click "Edit" next to the driver
   - Uncheck "Active"
   - Click "Save"

### Managing Locations

1. Click "Manage Locations" in the Management Dashboard
2. **Add a Location**:
   - Click "Add Location"
   - Enter location name, address, and description
   - Click "Save"
3. **Use Saved Locations**:
   - When creating a trip, select "Use Saved Location" for pickup or dropoff
   - Choose from your saved locations

### Assigning Trips to Drivers

**Single Trip Assignment:**
1. Find the trip in the trip list
2. Click "Assign" next to the trip
3. Select a driver from the dropdown
4. Click "Assign"
5. The driver will receive a notification

**Bulk Assignment:**
1. Check the boxes next to multiple trips
2. Click "Assign" button at the top
3. Select a driver from the dialog
4. Click "Assign"
5. All selected trips will be assigned to the driver

### Daily Assignment Emails

Send daily assignment summaries to all drivers:
1. Click "Send Daily Assignment Emails" in the Management Dashboard
2. Confirm sending emails
3. Emails will be sent to all eligible drivers
4. Drivers will receive a summary of their next day's assignments

## For Drivers

### Viewing Your Assignments

1. Log in to access your Driver Dashboard
2. You'll see all trips assigned to you
3. Trips are organized by date
4. Click on a trip card to see full details

### Trip Information

Each trip card shows:
- **Airport**: The airport for this trip
- **Flight Number**: The flight you're picking up
- **Pickup Date & Time**: When to pick up the passenger
- **Pickup Location**: Where to pick up
- **Dropoff Location**: Where to drop off
- **Passenger Information**: Name and contact details
- **Special Instructions**: Any special requirements

### Checking Flight Status

1. Find the trip in your dashboard
2. Click "Check Flight Status" on the trip card
3. The system will retrieve the latest flight information
4. **Note**: Excessive use of flight status checks may incur additional costs

### Notifications

You'll receive notifications when:
- A new trip is assigned to you
- A trip is reassigned from another driver
- Daily assignment summaries (if enabled)

**Notification Preferences:**
- You can set your preference in your driver profile:
  - Email Only
  - Both Email and In-App

### Daily Assignment Summary

If enabled by your administrator, you'll receive:
- **Email**: A summary of all trips for the following day
- Includes basic information; full details are available in the app

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Q: Can I change my notification preferences?

A: Yes! Contact your administrator to update your notification preferences (Email or Both).

### Q: What if a flight is delayed?

A: You can check the flight status using the "Check Flight Status" button on the trip card. Update the pickup time if needed.

### Q: How do I add a new location?

A: Go to Management Dashboard → "Manage Locations" → "Add Location". Enter the details and save.

### Q: Can I assign multiple trips at once?

A: Yes! Check the boxes next to multiple trips and click "Assign" to assign them all to one driver.

### Q: What happens if I can't complete a trip?

A: Contact your administrator or manager immediately. They can reassign the trip to another driver.

### Q: How do I view past trips?

A: Use the date filters in the Management Dashboard to view trips from previous dates.

### Q: Can I edit a trip after it's created?

A: Yes, administrators and managers can edit trips. Click "Edit" next to the trip in the trip list.

### Q: What information do I need to create a trip?

A: Minimum required information:
- Airport
- Flight number
- Pickup date and time
- Pickup location
- Dropoff location
- Passenger name

### Q: How do I know if a trip is assigned to me?

A: Assigned trips appear in your Driver Dashboard. You'll also receive a notification when assigned.

## Support

### Getting Help

If you need assistance:

1. **Check This Guide**: Many questions are answered in this documentation
2. **Contact Your Administrator**: For account issues, permissions, or company-specific questions
3. **Technical Support**: For technical issues or bugs, contact your system administrator

### Common Issues

**Can't log in:**
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Check that you've completed the sign-up process
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset your password

**Not seeing trips:**
- Verify you're assigned to trips
- Check your date filters
- Ensure you're viewing the correct dashboard

**Notifications not working:**
- Check your notification preferences in your profile
- Verify your email/phone number is correct
- Contact your administrator to update preferences

**Flight status not updating:**
- Flight status checks are manual - click "Check Flight Status"
- Some flights may not have real-time data available
- Contact support if issues persist
