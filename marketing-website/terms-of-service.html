8. SMS and Text Messaging Services

8.1 SMS Service Description

The Service may include SMS (text messaging) notifications for transactional communications, including booking confirmations, trip reminders, driver assignment updates, and trip completion notifications. SMS services are provided through Telnyx, a third-party SMS service provider.

8.2 Opt-In Requirement

SMS notifications require explicit opt-in consent. You may opt in to SMS notifications through:

  • Checking the opt-in checkbox during booking submission
  • Enabling SMS notifications in your account settings
  • Texting START, YES, SUBSCRIBE, or OPTIN to our designated SMS number

8.3 Opt-Out Rights

You may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, QUIT, END, or CANCEL to any SMS message
  • Disabling SMS notifications in your account settings

Opt-out requests are processed immediately. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

8.4 Message Frequency and Types

SMS messages are transactional in nature and may include:

  • Booking confirmations (sent immediately after booking submission)
  • Booking acceptance notifications (when a manager confirms your booking)
  • Trip reminders (24 hours and 1 hour before scheduled trips)
  • Driver status updates (when driver is en route or arrives)
  • Trip completion notifications

Message frequency varies based on your booking activity, with a maximum of 5-6 messages per booking cycle. We do not send marketing or promotional messages via SMS.
