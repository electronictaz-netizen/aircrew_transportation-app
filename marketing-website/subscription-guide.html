Subscription and Trial Guide

Everything you need to know about our subscription plans and free trial

Overview

The Onyx Transportation App offers flexible subscription plans to meet your business needs. All new companies can start with a 14-day free trial to explore all features before committing to a paid plan.

Free Trial

Starting Your Free Trial

When you sign up through the "Start Free Trial" link on our website, you automatically receive:

  • 14 days of full access
  • Basic tier features during the trial period
  • No credit card required
  • Automatic setup - no configuration needed

During Your Trial

  1. Trial Banner: You'll see a banner at the top of your dashboard showing days remaining and a direct link to subscription management.
  2. Full Feature Access: During your trial, you have access to all Basic tier features including unlimited trips, location management, custom fields, standard reports, and email support.
  3. Subscribe Anytime: You can subscribe at any point during your trial. Your subscription will begin immediately, and you'll be billed from that date.

Trial Expiration

  • 3 Days Before Expiration: You'll see a warning banner reminding you to subscribe
  • On Expiration: Your account automatically downgrades to the Free tier
  • After Expiration: You'll see an alert banner prompting you to subscribe to continue using premium features

Data Retention and Cancellation

What Happens to Your Data

We understand that your data is important to your business. Here's what you need to know about data retention:

During Active Subscription

  • All your data (trips, drivers, locations, reports, etc.) is stored securely and remains accessible
  • You can export your data at any time through the Service's export features

After Cancellation

  • Grace Period: You retain full access until the end of your current billing period
  • 30-Day Retention: Your data is retained for 30 days after your subscription ends, allowing you to reactivate and regain access
  • Data Export: We strongly recommend exporting your data before canceling your subscription
  • After 30 Days: Data may be archived or deleted in accordance with our Terms of Service

Account Deletion

If your account remains inactive for 90 days, we may permanently delete your account and all associated data. Once data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.

For complete details on data retention, deletion policies, and your rights, please review our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a credit card to start a free trial?
A: No, the free trial requires no credit card. You can explore all features risk-free.
Q: What happens if I don't subscribe before my trial ends?
A: Your account automatically moves to the Free tier. You can subscribe at any time to regain premium features.
Q: Can I change plans anytime?
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through Subscription Management.
Q: When will I be charged?
A: You're charged immediately when you subscribe. After that, you're billed monthly on the same date.
Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
Q: What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?
A: After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days to allow for account reactivation. We recommend exporting your data before canceling. After 30 days, data may be archived or deleted in accordance with our Terms of Service. Please see our Terms of Service for complete details on data retention and deletion policies.
