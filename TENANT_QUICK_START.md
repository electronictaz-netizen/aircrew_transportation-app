# Quick Start Guide for New Companies

Welcome! This quick guide will get you up and running in 5 minutes.

## Step 1: Receive Your Invitation

Your company administrator will send you an invitation email. **Keep this email** - you'll need it to sign up.

## Step 2: Create Your Account

1. Click the link in your invitation email
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your email (the one from the invitation)
4. Create a password
5. Complete sign-up

## Step 3: Log In

1. Go to the application URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard

## Common Tasks

### Creating a Trip (Administrators/Managers)

1. Click "Create Trip"
2. Select Airport (BUF, ROC, SYR, or ALB)
3. Enter Flight Number
4. Set Pickup Date & Time
5. Choose Pickup Location (Airport, Saved Location, or Custom)
6. Choose Dropoff Location
7. Enter Passenger Information
8. Click "Save"

### Assigning a Trip to a Driver (Administrators/Managers)

1. Find the trip in the list
2. Click "Assign" next to the trip
3. Select a driver
4. Click "Assign"
5. Driver receives notification automatically

### Checking Flight Status (Drivers)

1. Find the trip in your dashboard
2. Click "Check Flight Status" on the trip card
3. View the latest flight information
