## Getting Started

### What is the Customer Portal?

The Customer Portal is a secure, self-service website where you can:
- ‚úÖ View your upcoming trips
- ‚úÖ See your trip history
- ‚úÖ Track your driver's location when your trip is in progress
- ‚úÖ Download receipts and invoices
- ‚úÖ Request changes to upcoming trips
- ‚úÖ Rate and review completed trips

### How to Access the Portal

1. **Get Your Portal Link**
   - Your transportation provider will send you a link
   - Or visit: **https://onyxdispatch.us/portal/[CODE]**
   - Replace `[CODE]` with your provider's booking code

2. **Bookmark the Link**
   - Save the portal link for easy access
   - You'll use the same link each time

## Logging In

### Step 1: Enter Your Information

1. **Open the portal link** in your web browser
2. **Enter your email address** OR **phone number**
   - Use the email or phone number on file with your transportation provider
   - You only need to enter one (email OR phone)
3. **Click "Continue"**

### Step 2: Verify Access Code

1. **Check your email or phone** for an access code
   - The code is 6 characters (letters and numbers)
   - Example: `A3B9X2`
2. **Enter the access code** in the portal
3. **Click "Verify Code"**

### Troubleshooting Login

**"No account found"**
- Verify you're using the correct email/phone number
- Contact your transportation provider to ensure portal access is enabled
- Check for typos in your email/phone

**"Invalid access code"**
- Access codes can only be used once
- Request a new code by clicking "Back" and trying again
- Check that you entered the code correctly (case-sensitive)

**Didn't receive access code?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email/phone number is correct
- Contact your transportation provider

## Viewing Your Trips

### Upcoming Trips

1. **Click "Upcoming Trips" tab** (default view)
2. **View your scheduled trips**
   - Each trip card shows:
     - Flight number or trip identifier
     - Date and time
     - Pickup and dropoff locations
     - Number of passengers
     - Driver name (if assigned)
     - Trip status

### Trip History

1. **Click "Trip History" tab**
2. **View your past trips**
   - See all completed trips
   - Same information as upcoming trips
   - Access receipts and ratings

### Trip Statuses

- **Unassigned**: Trip created but no driver assigned yet
- **Assigned**: Driver has been assigned
- **In Progress**: Trip is currently happening (live tracking available)
- **Completed**: Trip has been finished

## Live Driver Tracking

When your trip status is **In Progress**, you can see your driver's location on a map.

### How It Works

1. **Go to "Upcoming Trips"** and find the trip that is in progress
2. **Live Driver Location** appears on the trip card
3. **A small map** shows the driver's current position
4. **Location updates** automatically every few seconds

### What You'll See

- **"Live driver location"** heading with the time of the last update
- **Map** with a marker showing where your driver is
- If the driver hasn't started tracking yet, you'll see: *"Driver is on the way. Location will appear when the driver starts tracking."*

### Tips

- Keep the portal open on your phone or computer to watch the driver approach
- Location is approximate and may lag slightly
- If the map doesn't appear, refresh the page or check back in a moment

## Downloading Receipts

### How to Download

1. **Go to "Trip History" tab**
2. **Find the trip** you want a receipt for
3. **Click "üìÑ Download Receipt"**
4. **Receipt will download** as an HTML file
5. **Open the file** in any web browser
6. **Print or save** as needed

### Receipt Information

Your receipt includes:
- Customer information (name, email, phone)
- Trip details (date, time, locations)
- Trip ID and status
- Number of passengers
- Pricing information (if available)
- Company information

### Printing Receipts

1. **Open the downloaded receipt** in your browser
2. **Press Ctrl+P** (Windows) or **Cmd+P** (Mac)
3. **Select your printer**
4. **Click Print**

## Requesting Trip Changes

### When Can You Request Changes?

- ‚úÖ Only for **upcoming trips**
- ‚úÖ Only if trip status is **not "Completed"**
- ‚úÖ Changes must be requested in advance

### How to Request Changes

1. **Go to "Upcoming Trips" tab**
2. **Find the trip** you want to modify
3. **Click "‚úèÔ∏è Request Changes"**
4. **Select change type**:
   - **Date**: Change the pickup date
   - **Time**: Change the pickup time
   - **Location**: Change pickup or dropoff location
   - **Passengers**: Change number of passengers
   - **Other**: Any other change
5. **Describe the changes** you need
6. **Provide a reason** for the change
7. **Click "Submit Request"**

### What Happens Next?

1. **Request is submitted** to your transportation provider
2. **Provider reviews** your request
3. **Provider contacts you** to confirm or discuss changes
4. **Trip is updated** if approved

### Important Notes

- ‚ö†Ô∏è Changes are **not automatic** - they must be approved
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Contact your provider **as soon as possible** for urgent changes
- ‚ö†Ô∏è Some changes may not be possible (e.g., same-day time changes)

## Rating Your Trips

### How to Rate a Trip

1. **Go to "Trip History" tab**
2. **Find a completed trip** you want to rate
3. **Click "‚≠ê Rate Trip"**
4. **Rate your experience**:
   - **Overall Rating**: 1-5 stars (required)
   - **Driver Rating**: 1-5 stars (if driver was assigned)
   - **Review**: Optional written feedback
   - **Would Recommend**: Check if you'd recommend the service
5. **Click "Submit Rating"**

### Rating Guidelines

- **5 Stars**: Excellent service, exceeded expectations
- **4 Stars**: Very good service, minor issues
- **3 Stars**: Good service, met expectations
- **2 Stars**: Below expectations, some issues
- **1 Star**: Poor service, significant problems

### Your Feedback Matters

- Ratings help improve service quality
- Reviews help other customers
- Feedback is shared with drivers and management
- All feedback is appreciated!

## Troubleshooting

### Portal Won't Load

**Check:**
- Internet connection
- Portal link is correct (e.g. **https://onyxdispatch.us/portal/YOURCODE**)
- Browser is up to date
- Try a different browser

**Solution:**
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito/private mode
- Contact your transportation provider

### Can't See My Trips

**Possible reasons:**
- No trips assigned to your account
- Trips are for a different customer
- Portal access not enabled

**Solution:**
- Contact your transportation provider
- Verify your email/phone matches their records
- Ask them to check your account

### Receipt Won't Download

**Check:**
- Browser pop-up blocker is disabled
- Downloads are allowed
- Sufficient storage space

**Solution:**
- Allow pop-ups for the portal site
- Check browser download settings
- Try a different browser

### Can't Request Changes

**Possible reasons:**
- Trip is already completed
- Trip is in progress
- Request feature not available

**Solution:**
- Only upcoming trips can be modified
- Contact provider directly for urgent changes
- Use phone/email for immediate needs

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I change my email/phone in the portal?**
A: No, contact your provider to update your information.

**Q: How long are access codes valid?**
A: Access codes expire after use. Request a new one if needed.

**Q: Can I cancel a trip through the portal?**
A: No, contact your provider directly to cancel trips.

**Q: Can I book new trips through the portal?**
A: No, the portal is for viewing existing trips only. Use your provider's booking link (e.g. **https://onyxdispatch.us/booking/[CODE]**) or contact your provider for new bookings.
